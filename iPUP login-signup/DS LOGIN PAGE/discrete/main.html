South Wing

- The South Wing Academic Building complements the infrastructure of PUP's Main Academic Building, providing additional space for classrooms, laboratories, faculty offices, and student services. This extension enhances the capacity of the university to accommodate the growing needs of its academic community, ensuring a conducive environment for teaching and learning.

Interfaith Chapel

- The Interfaith Chapel at PUP is a place of worship and reflection where individuals from various religious backgrounds can come together to practice their faith, engage in spiritual activities, and find solace. It serves as a symbol of unity, diversity, and tolerance within the university community, promoting interfaith dialogue and understanding.

Nutrition and Food Science Building

- The College of Nutrition & Food Science Building houses the academic departments, laboratories, research facilities, and administrative offices related to nutrition, food science, and related disciplines. It provides students with state-of-the-art facilities for hands-on learning, research opportunities, and practical training in the field of food and nutrition.

Campus Development and Maintenance Building

- The Campus Development & Maintenance Department Building serves as the administrative headquarters for overseeing the planning, construction, maintenance, and beautification of the PUP campus. It plays a crucial role in ensuring the infrastructure and facilities meet the needs of students, faculty, and staff, while also preserving the aesthetic appeal and functionality of the campus environment.

PUP Sta. Mesa Ferry Station

- The PUP Ferry Station provides transportation access for students, faculty, and staff commuting to and from the university via waterways. Located strategically along a river or coastline, the ferry station offers a convenient and efficient mode of transportation, particularly for those living in areas accessible by water routes.

Apolinario Mabini Shrine

- The fourth relocation site of the Mabini Shrine year 2010 and is declared as its permanent home under the National Historical Institute. It features the things that are once owned by Apolinario Mabini and other memorabilia

Gazebo

- A shaded space at the PUP oval and serves as a tambayan. It gives a picturesque spot for students to enjoy the campus environment and connect with co-Iskos in a tranquil setting.

Grandstand

- A vast seating area located at the PUP oval. The grandstand is the focal point in most university events and contributes to the overall atmosphere of excitement and camaraderie within the campus space.

Track and Football Oval

- The PUP Oval serves as a venue for jogging or casual strolls, as well as the training ground for football athletes. With its expansive area, it provides opportunities for individuals of diverse fitness levels to maintain an active and healthy lifestyle. Also, it serves as a site for hosting grand campus events and celebrations.

Community Building (Gabriela Silang)

- A building built in 2011 paying homage to a fearless hero, Gabriela Silang. This building is intended for student activities, and the student offices in various colleges, such as the Student Center and Student Council Offices

PUP Obelisk

- Long-standing at the core of the campus, it symbolizes the enduring strength of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines as a beacon of higher education, promoting educational and moral aims. Committed to the future, the obelisk stands as an emblem of strength and greatness, immutable against the passage of time

Outdoor Basketball/Volleyball and Tennis Court

- The Outdoor Basketball and Volleyball court are Currently under construction. Envisioned to be an outdoor gym fit for basketball, tennis, and other Palarong Pambansa sports. PE classes will also be held in this school facility. While the Tennis court is a go-to destination for tennis at any skill level, where one can enjoy friendly matches or hone their skills in a welcoming environment designed for fun and fitness. This venue is tailored to accommodate Tennis PE classes as well, providing ample opportunities for both recreational play and skill development.

Pylon

- A triad of pillars symbolizing wisdom, strength, and beauty is upheld by the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. It advocates that there should be wisdom to contrive, strength to support, and beauty to adorn all significant endeavors.

Gymnasium and Sports Center

- University gymnasium that was rebranded as the PUP Multipurpose Building and Sports Development Facility. It caters commercial spaces, events, and sports activities and has the capacity to accommodate 2,000 people. It also serves as the training ground for the student athletes that participate in the intercollegiate sports program.

Swimming Pool

- An olympic-sized swimming pool that is only available here in the Mabini Campus of PUP. It is capable of handling several swimming classes and student athlete activities simultaneously, enhancing the campusâ€™ sports and recreational offerings.

Tahanan ng Alumni

- A place for PUP alumni to connect, network, and engage with the alma mater. It houses the resources and support for alumni activities, events, and initiatives. Additionally, the Alumni Grounds serves as a space where alumni gather for reunions and meetings.

Physical Education Building

- Facility dedicated to physical education classes. It houses the faculty offices, and various amenities such as the studios and equipment storage areas.

Ninoy Aquino Library and Learning Resources Center

- The Ninoy Aquino Learning Resource Center, located within the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP), serves as a hub for educational resources, including books, journals, multimedia materials, and online databases. Named after the Filipino senator and democracy icon Benigno "Ninoy" Aquino Jr., it embodies a commitment to knowledge, learning, and the pursuit of academic excellence.

Amphitheater

- The Amphitheater at PUP is a dynamic space designed for hosting performances, lectures, seminars, and other cultural events. Its open-air design allows for large audiences to gather comfortably while enjoying performances or engaging in academic discourse. The Amphitheater serves as a vibrant hub of artistic and intellectual exchange within the university community.

Laboratory High School

The High School Prefabricated (Prefab) Building accommodates the academic needs of high school students enrolled in PUP's affiliated secondary education program. Constructed using prefabricated materials, it provides classrooms, laboratories, and facilities tailored to the educational requirements of secondary-level students, ensuring a conducive learning environment.

Charlie del Rosario Hall

- The Charlie Del Rosario Hall serves as the center for student guidance and counseling services at PUP. Named after an esteemed figure in the university's history, it offers various support services to students, including academic advising, career counseling, personal counseling, and guidance on student affairs and welfare.
